Resume Analyzer is a web-based application designed to help students and job seekers evaluate their resumes using Applicant Tracking System (ATS) principles.

🔍 Resume Analysis: Upload your resume in TXT or DOCX format to receive an ATS score based on structure, keywords, and content quality.

🧠 Skill Extraction: The system automatically identifies technical skills present in your resume and highlights missing or weak areas.

📊 ATS Score & Suggestions: Get actionable improvement suggestions to increase your resume’s compatibility with modern hiring systems.

🌙 User Experience: The platform includes secure login, profile management and past report tracking for better usability.

Use the navigation menu above to upload resumes, view reports, and manage your profile.
