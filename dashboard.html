Resume Analyzer is a web-based application that helps students and job seekers evaluate their resumes using Applicant Tracking System (ATS) principles.

Resume Analysis

Upload resumes in TXT or DOCX format to receive an ATS score based on resume structure, keywords, and content quality.

Skill Extraction

The system automatically identifies technical skills present in the resume and highlights missing or weak skill areas.

ATS Score & Suggestions

Users receive clear improvement suggestions to increase resume compatibility with modern hiring systems.

User Experience

The platform includes secure login, profile management, and past report tracking to ensure smooth and organized usage.
